Automated Testing

The Why

Every company developing a product should have tests in place. Testing is an essential part of software development, especially to guarantee quality. Many smaller and mid-sized companies don't give testing much attention even though it's essential for delivering a strong product.

The How

Just like it's important to have tests in place, it also matters how we run these tests.
Save time and costs with mobile testing automation. In software development, every minute counts. Even though transitioning to testing automation is not straight forward, in the long run it will results in beneficiary results.

The Benefits

Production Stability

Mobile testing will allow you to go to production safely. Stop spending lots of hours in manual testing and coordination effort of the team to deploy to production.

Custom Reports

Automated tests will give you confidence by providing reports in the format you feel it is more convinient that will probably include:
  • version tested
  • environment
  • tests executed
  • test status
  • pass rate
...and quantitative data that will help you in decision making

In conclusion...

Making sure your mobile App runs in all kinds of devices: iOS, Android and it's respective OS versions. Having a good testability matrix is vital for reducing error rates in production which will result in better customer experience.
